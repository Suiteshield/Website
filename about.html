About SuiteShield

A locally owned plumbing company built on clean work, honest pricing and systems that simply keep working.

Who We Are

Your home's water systems, in good hands

SuiteShield Plumbing Systems is a locally owned and licensed plumbing company serving Woodstock, Ingersoll, London and the surrounding communities of southwestern Ontario.

We specialise in the systems that keep a home comfortable and protected — water heaters, smart leak detection, water treatment and the piping that ties it all together. It's detailed, behind-the-scenes work, and we believe it should be done with the same care whether anyone sees it or not.

The shield in our name is a promise: your home, protected by plumbing you can rely on. That's the standard we hold ourselves to on every job, big or small.
