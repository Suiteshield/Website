Our Plumbing Services

Complete water system installation, repair and renovation work — handled start to finish by a team that takes pride in tidy, lasting results.

Water Heating

Water Heaters & Tankless Systems

A reliable supply of hot water is something you should never have to think about. We supply, install and replace both tankless and traditional tank water heaters — and help you choose the right size and type for your household.

  • Tankless (on-demand) water heater installation
  • Traditional tank heater supply & replacement
  • Right-sizing for your home's hot water demand
  • Old unit removal & clean, code-conscious install
Smart Protection

Smart Water Leak Detection

A small leak can do big, expensive damage before you ever notice it. We install smart leak detection and automatic shut-off systems that monitor your plumbing around the clock and stop water before it becomes a problem. Many Canadian insurers recognize these systems and may offer a premium discount once one is installed.

  • Smart leak sensor supply & installation
  • Automatic whole-home water shut-off valves
  • Wireless leak sensors with instant phone alerts
  • Freeze & burst-pipe early warning
Water Quality

Water Treatment & Filtration

Hard water and impurities are tough on your home, your appliances and your skin. We install water softeners and whole-home filtration systems so every tap delivers cleaner, better water.

  • Water softener installation
  • Whole-home filtration systems
  • Drinking water & specialty treatment options
  • Advice on the right setup for your water
